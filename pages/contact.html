Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download our mobile app from the App Store or Google Play, then tap "Sign Up" to create your free account.

Is Zellion free to use?

Yes! Zellion is completely free for buyers and renters. We earn commission from successful transactions with real estate professionals.

How do I contact a real estate agent?

Simply tap on any property listing and you'll see options to contact the listing agent directly through our platform.

Can I save properties for later?

Absolutely! Create an account and use the heart icon on any listing to save it to your favorites.

Do you offer virtual tours?

Yes! Many of our listings include 360° virtual tours and high-quality photo galleries.

How often are listings updated?

Our listings are updated in real-time, ensuring you always see the most current available properties.
